scratch.git privacy policy

Last updated June 26, 2024

As scratch.git is meant to be part of an ecosystem primarily for young children, it is important to point out that we respect your privacy (as a tiny passion project, we seriously don't care about your data). Regardless, this document outlines places where your data may be collected or disclosed.

scratch.git: the main component of our project: https://github.com/ajskateboarder/scratch-git.

Git: a version control system used by scratch.git to keep track of project versions: https://git-scm.com/

repository: a local or online storage containing all versions of a project

scratch.git

Third Parties

By using scratch.git, you agree to be bound by TurboWarp Desktop's privacy policy.

When you clone, pull from, or push to a repository, you are subject to the privacy policy of the site hosting that repository. For example, when pushing to GitHub, you are subject to GitHub's privacy policy.

As scratch.git makes use of icons provided by Cloudflare's CDN, you are subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy and anonymous usage data may be collected by the service, as stated by the "What about the privacy of my users?" section of the CDN homepage.

Authentication

When authenticating with scratch.git to push to a GitHub repository, we will store and reuse a temporary user access token locally only for authentication purposes. When authenticating to push to other online repositories, Git will prompt you for your username and password. This information is used once, not stored anywhere, and is only for authentication purposes.

Preferences

All preferences for viewing diffs (e.g., using plain text, using highlights) for scratch.git are stored in the application and are not shared with third parties. Preferences persist after reloading TurboWarp Desktop, but can be cleared from the browser console.

Preferences for committing to repositories (e.g., name, email address) are only shared with Git and used for no other purpose.

Project Viewer

Refers to our viewer for projects made using scratch.git: https://ajskateboarder.github.io/scratch-git.

Third Parties

As scratch.git makes use of icons provided by Cloudflare's CDN, you are subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy and anonymous usage data may be collected by the service.

When you load a project from an online repository, you are subject to the privacy policy of the site hosting the repository.

By using https://ajskateboarder.github.io, you agree to be bound by GitHub's privacy policy.
